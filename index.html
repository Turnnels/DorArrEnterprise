About DorArr Enterprise

Founded in 2015, DorArr Enterprise Uganda Ltd has grown to become a trusted name in retail and service provision across Uganda. Our mission is to provide high-quality products and exceptional services that enhance our customers' lifestyles and businesses.

Located along Kawempe–Tula Road, we serve customers from all over Uganda with our diverse range of products and comprehensive service offerings.
